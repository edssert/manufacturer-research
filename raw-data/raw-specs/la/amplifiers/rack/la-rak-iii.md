# LA-RAK III (L-Acoustics, Rack)

> **원문** — 사용자가 제공한 원문 스펙 텍스트 그대로 보관.

## Overview

- MILAN-Certified Redundancy
- Touring rack with 3 LA7.16
- Architecture: 48 channels of amplification
- Output power: Up to 62,400 W at 8 ohms
- Power supply: Three-phase 32 A IEC in/link, 30 A NEMA for 110 V
- Inputs: Milan-AVB, AES/EBU, Analog
- Network: Milan-certified with seamless network redundancy
