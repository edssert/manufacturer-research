# K2 프리셋 구성 (Variable Curvature WST systems)

> 원본 출처: `preset_guide_EN.pdf` p.51-52 (owner's manual EN v29.0)
> 상위 references 폴더(`../../preset-guide-en.pdf`)에 전체 원본 PDF 보관.

| Loudspeaker configuration | K2 | K1-SB | KS28/SB28 | CS1 | Acoustic properties |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| K2 line source | [K2 xxx] | — | — | — | 35 Hz - 20 kHz, adjustable horizontal directivity |
| K2 + K1-SB (on top) | [K2 xxx] | [K1SB_X K2] | — | — | enhanced LF throw, down to 30 Hz |
| K2 + coupled K1-SB (on top/beside/behind) | [K2 xxx] | [K1SB_60] | — | — | reinforced LF contour, LF rejection(side polarized or rear cardioid), down to 33 Hz |
| K2 + coupled K1-SB (behind, NC) | [K2 xxx] | [K1SB_100_NC] | — | — | reinforced LF contour, LF rejection(rear cardioid), down to 25 Hz |
| K2 + subwoofers | [K2 xxx] | — | [xx28_60] | — | reinforced LF contour, down to 25 Hz |
| K2 + coupled CS1 (beside/behind) | [K2 xxx] | — | — | [CS1_60] | reinforced LF contour, LF rejection(rear cardioid), down to 25 Hz |
| K2 + coupled CS1 (supercardioid) | [K2 xxx] | — | — | [CS1_60_S] | reinforced LF contour, supercardioid pattern, down to 25 Hz |

`[K2 xxx]`의 xxx = 70/90/110 (adjustable directivity) — K2 조정핀을 선택한
프리셋과 반드시 일치시킬 것. 카디오이드 배열 시 `[xx28_60_C]`/`[xx28_60_Cx]` 사용.

Downfill 옵션: K2 자체가 Kara/Kara II의 downfill 소스로도 쓰임(K1.md 참고).
