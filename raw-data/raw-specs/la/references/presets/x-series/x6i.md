# X6i 프리셋 구성 (Coaxial loudspeaker enclosures)

> 원본 출처: `preset_guide_EN.pdf` p.80 (owner's manual EN v29.0)
> 상위 references 폴더(`../../preset-guide-en.pdf`)에 전체 원본 PDF 보관.

X6i는 패시브 코액시얼 인클로저(원문 명시).

| Loudspeaker configuration | X6i preset | SB6i/SB10i | Syva Sub | Acoustic properties |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| single enclosure [X6i] | [X6i] | — | — | 69 Hz - 20 kHz |
| single enclosure [X6i_50] | [X6i_50] | — | — | 54 Hz - 20 kHz |
| single enclosure (low latency) | [X6i_MO] | — | — | 65 Hz - 20 kHz, low latency |
| + closely coupled subwoofers | [X6i]/[X6i_50] | [SBxx_200] | — | down to 32 Hz(SB6i)/29 Hz(SB10i), reinforced LF contour |
| + coupled subwoofers | [X6i]/[X6i_50] | [SBxx_100] | — | down to 29 Hz(SB6i)/27 Hz(SB10i), reinforced LF contour |
| + separated subwoofers | [X6i_50] | — | [SYVA SUB_60] | down to 26 Hz |

`[xx_MO]` 저지연 관련 안내는 a-series/x4i 문서와 동일.

출력 라우팅: `[X6i]`/`[X6i_50]`/`[X6i_MO]` 모두 OUT1/2=PA(IN A) + OUT3/4=PA(IN B).
